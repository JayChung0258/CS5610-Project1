My Culinary Adventures

A take on my journey of cooking in the U.S. and my relentless efforts to improve – mainly in hopes of not starving.

Dumplings with a Side of Garlic & Soy Sauce Art

These homemade dumplings may not win any beauty contests, but they’re filled with love...and a hint of survival instinct. The garlic and soy sauce arrangement was unintentional, yet surprisingly artistic!

Stir-Fried Noodles with a Side of Steamed Broccoli

A well-balanced meal featuring noodles and veggies! The broccoli might look slightly overpowered, but hey, at least it’s green. Slowly leveling up from instant noodles to actual "dishes."

Sizzling Chicken with Onions

A culinary masterpiece in the making! The sizzling sound may suggest pro-level cooking, but let’s be honest, I’m still working on not overcooking the onions. Slowly transforming into a home chef…or so I hope!

Crispy Fried Chicken Wings with a Classic Coke

Nothing says "I’m learning to cook" like homemade fried chicken! Perfectly paired with a cold Coke, this dish is my attempt to bring restaurant vibes home. Just trying to make sure I don’t set off the smoke alarm.
